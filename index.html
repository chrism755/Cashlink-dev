How do I activate my account? +

Pay the activation fee of KES 150 via M-PESA to +254706494401 (MARY MPURUNGA) or PayPal to marympurunga@gmail.com. Then enter your transaction ID on the activation page and wait 2–5 minutes for admin approval.

How does the referral system work? +

Level 1 referrals (people you personally invite): KSh 80 each. Level 2 (their referrals): KSh 20 each. Level 3 (Level 2's referrals): KSh 10 each. All referral earnings go to your Account Balance.

What is the minimum cashout? +

The minimum withdrawal is KES 150. Transaction fees apply: KES 15 (150–350), KES 30 (350–700), KES 50 (700–1,500), KES 70 (1,500–3,000), KES 150 (3,000–10,000). Processed within 24 hours.

What is the Karibu Bonus? +

The Karibu Bonus is KES 100 awarded when you successfully refer your first 20 active members. It's a one-time welcome bonus you can collect from the Karibu Bonus section.

How much do I earn from videos? +

TikTok videos: KSh 15 each (3 per day). Instagram reels: KSh 20 each (3 per day). YouTube videos: KSh 25 each (3 per day). Max daily video earnings: KSh 180/day from videos alone!

What is the Weekly Bonus? +

If you invite 50 new members within a single week, you receive a KES 200 Weekly Bonus. This resets every Monday.

Can I join from outside Kenya? +

Yes! Members from Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Nigeria, Ghana and other countries are welcome. International members pay via PayPal ($4 USD activation).
